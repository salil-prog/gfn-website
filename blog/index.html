Introducing CookLog — and an invitation to help us test it

We're introducing CookLog, a lightweight, offline-first app for logging household cooking energy use. It lets a household record cooking events and fuel purchases, then turns those logs into a clear picture of what cooking actually costs — weekly and monthly spend broken down by fuel, built from real purchases rather than guesswork. Built in the spirit of the MECS cooking diaries agenda, and designed for intermittent connectivity, shared phones and entry-level Android devices.

Become a CookLog early tester

We're selecting a small, hands-on cohort of early testers across Kenya and India to try CookLog in real kitchens for a few weeks and tell us where it falls short.
